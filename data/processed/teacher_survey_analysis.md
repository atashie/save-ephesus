# Teacher Survey Analysis - Ephesus Elementary

**Source:** NC Teacher Working Conditions Survey 2024
**Respondents:** 43 teachers at Ephesus Elementary
**Comparison:** CHCCS District (1,017 respondents), NC State

---

## Executive Summary

The 2024 NC Teacher Working Conditions Survey reveals that **Ephesus Elementary has exceptional school climate metrics** that far exceed district averages, particularly in student behavior, teacher satisfaction, and community engagement. Despite known facility challenges, teachers report Ephesus as a positive and effective learning environment.

### Key Findings

| Metric | Ephesus | District | Difference |
|--------|---------|----------|------------|
| Students follow conduct rules | **97.67%** | 68.83% | **+29 pts** |
| Good place to work and learn | **97.67%** | 91.25% | **+6 pts** |
| Physical conflicts among students | **6.98%** | 36.38% | **-29 pts** |
| Parents know what's going on | **97.67%** | 84.86% | **+13 pts** |

---

## 1. Student Behavior & Conduct (Strongest Finding)

Ephesus teachers report exceptional student behavior—the strongest differentiation from district averages.

### Positive Conduct Metrics

| Metric | Ephesus | District | State | Difference vs District |
|--------|---------|----------|-------|------------------------|
| Students follow conduct rules | **97.67%** | 68.83% | 68.48% | **+28.84 pts** |
| Uses positive behavioral interventions | **100%** | 80.53% | 83.88% | **+19.47 pts** |
| Teachers enforce rules | **95.35%** | 79.74% | 84.15% | **+15.61 pts** |
| Leadership supports discipline | **88.37%** | 69.03% | 79.38% | **+19.34 pts** |
| Leadership enforces rules | **86.05%** | 63.82% | 76.94% | **+22.23 pts** |

### Behavioral Problems (Lower = Better)

| Issue | Ephesus | District | State | Note |
|-------|---------|----------|-------|------|
| Bullying | **18.60%** | 44.74% | 47.81% | Less than half district rate |
| Physical conflicts | **6.98%** | 36.38% | 43.37% | **5x fewer than district** |
| Threats toward teachers | **0%** | 15.54% | 16.91% | **Zero incidents** |
| Student disrespect of teachers | **30.23%** | 57.82% | 62.82% | Nearly half district rate |
| Cyberbullying | **0%** | 27.43% | 33.76% | **Zero incidents** |
| Cheating | **2.33%** | 31.56% | 37.16% | Near zero |
| Drug/tobacco use | **0%** | 28.42% | 34.63% | **Zero incidents** |
| Weapons possession | **0%** | 9.05% | 9.62% | **Zero incidents** |
| Gang activity | **0%** | 5.31% | 11.08% | **Zero incidents** |
| Robbery/theft | **2.33%** | 16.62% | 20.06% | Near zero |
| Tardiness/skipping | **16.28%** | 60.47% | 57.41% | 3.7x better |

**Narrative:** Ephesus has the lowest behavioral problem rates in the district. Teachers report **zero incidents** in multiple categories (violence threats, cyberbullying, weapons, drugs, gang activity) where district averages are significant.

## Summary: Why This Matters for the Closure Decision

### The Data Shows Ephesus Is:

1. **A safe school** - Zero reported incidents of violence threats, cyberbullying, weapons, drugs, or gang activity

2. **A well-managed school** - 98% agree students follow conduct rules (29 pts above district)

3. **A school teachers want to work at** - 98% say it's a good place to work (6 pts above district)

4. **A community-connected school** - 98% say parents know what's going on (13 pts above district)

5. **An equitable school** - Basic needs met at 86% despite Title I status (8 pts above district)
